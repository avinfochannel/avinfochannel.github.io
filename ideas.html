Shadow Play

Wherever you have shadows there is light. You simply can’t have shadows without light. They can create drama, depth and mystery in an image. Their high contrast is ideal for black and white composition as well as abstracts.

Shadow Attention

The human eye can be drawn into an image via shadows. You can utilize shadows to redirect the viewer’s attention and create a counterpoint for the subject. This image would not work without the shadow.

Train your eyes to look for the shadows. The effort is worth it as they create drama, depth and mystery in an image.
